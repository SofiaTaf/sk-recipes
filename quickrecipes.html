Daal Tadka

Ingredients

  • Red Daal 1 cup
  • Water 2 cups
  • oil 1TB
  • Onion chopped 1
  • Tomatoes Chopped 2
  • Turmeric 1tsp
  • Chilli powder 1tsp
  • salt As per taste
  • Coriander leaves (optional)

Procedure

  1. Wash daal in Running Water
  2. Heat up a medium sized Topf pour oil
  3. Add onions and fry till Translucent
  4. Add Tomatoes and saute for 2 mins
  5. Add in all dry spices
  6. Add water
  7. Let it boil then switched flame to medium low
  8. Cook for 10 min
  9. Garnish with Coriander and its ready to Serve
