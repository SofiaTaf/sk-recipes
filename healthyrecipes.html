chikpea salad

Ingredients

  • Boiled Chickpeas 1 cup
  • vinegar 1TBS
  • oil 1TBs
  • Onion chopped 1
  • Tomatoes Chopped 2
  • Parsley chopped
  • pepper powder powder 1tsp
  • salt As per taste
  • Coriander leaves (optional)

Procedure

  1. Mix oil,vinegar with salt and pepper
  2. Mix remaining ingredients together
  3. Drizzle oil mixture over it
  4. Mix well
  5. Enjoy!
